About Crochet

Crochet is a craft where yarn is turned into fabric using a crochet hook. It can be used to make clothes, toys, bags, blankets, and decorative items.

Materials Needed

  • Crochet Hook
  • Yarn
  • Scissors
  • Measuring Tape
  • Stitch Markers
